Introduction

Cats are small, carnivorous mammals that have been companions to humans for thousands of years.

They are known for their independence, agility, and affectionate behavior toward their owners.

// Example: A cat's purr is a sign of comfort or communication.

Breeds

There are over 70 cat breeds recognized by different organizations like CFA and TICA.

Each breed has its own traits like coat type, personality, and vocal tendencies.

Behavior

Cats communicate through meowing, purring, hissing, and body language.

Scratching, kneading, and tail flicks are common feline expressions.

cat.mood === "playful"
  • Purring indicates comfort
  • Hissing signals fear or aggression
Care

Proper care includes feeding, grooming, and routine vet visits.

Cats need mental stimulation, toys, and clean litter boxes.

feed(cat, food) → happyCat;
  • Provide fresh water daily
  • Brush their fur weekly
Fun Facts

Cats sleep for 12–16 hours a day and are most active during dawn and dusk.

The oldest known cat lived to be 38 years old!
